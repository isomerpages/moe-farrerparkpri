    <span><u><strong>What do students learn during CCE lessons?</strong></u></span>
<br><br>CCE lessons are anchored on three big ideas: Identity, Relationships, and Choices.
		<br>From these big ideas, students learn and demonstrate calues and social-emotional competencies through six content areas:
		<ul style="margin-left: 50px; padding-left: 0;">
			<li style="margin-bottom: 2px;">Cyber Wellness</li>
			<li style="margin-bottom: 2px;">Education and Career Guidance</li>
			<li style="margin-bottom: 2px;">Family Education</li>
			<li style="margin-bottom: 2px;">Mental Health</li>
			<li style="margin-bottom: 2px;">National Education</li>
			<li style="margin-bottom: 2px;">Sexuality Education</li>
		</ul>
