### Education and Career Guidance (ECG) 
<div style="background-color: #d2e1ce; border-radius: 10px; padding: 12px; font-size: 17px;">
    <p>The goals of ECG (Figure 1) are to support students in:</p>
    <ul style="margin: 5px 0 0 40px;">
        <li>
            <b>Discovering purpose – Who am I?</b><br>
            Nurture students' self-awareness to help them discover how they can meaningfully contribute to their community.
        </li>
        <li>
            <b>Exploring opportunities – Where do I want to go?</b><br>
            Develop students' self-directedness and confidence to explore and leverage educational and career opportunities while respecting the value of all occupations.
        </li>
        <li>
            <b>Staying relevant – How do I get there?</b><br>
            Build students' adaptability and resilience to embrace lifelong learning.
        </li>
    </ul>
</div>
