<span style="font-size:16px;"><strong><u>Learning Outcomes</u></strong><br>
Guided by the MOE Art Curriculum, students learn to:</span>

<ul style="margin:4px 0; padding-left:18px; line-height:1.3;">
<li><span style="font-size:16px;">Find joy and meaning in art-making</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:16px;">Communicate ideas visually with confidence</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:16px;">Connect art to culture, identity and everyday life</span></li>
</ul>
</font>
</td>

<td bgcolor="d2e1ce" style="border: 1px solid white;">
<font size="3">
<span style="font-size:16px;"><strong><u>Curriculum Approach</u></strong><br>
Our curriculum is anchored in Seeing, Expressing and Appreciating:</span>
<ul style="margin:4px 0; padding-left:18px; line-height:1.3;">
<li><span style="font-size:16px;"><strong>Seeing</strong> – Observing the world with curiosity and awareness</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:16px;"><strong>Expressing</strong> – Exploring materials and ideas through creative processes</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:16px;"><strong>Appreciating</strong> – Reflecting on artworks and diverse perspectives</span></li>
</ul>
<span style="font-size:16px;">
Through these experiences, students develop critical thinking, creativity, collaboration and empathy, while building resilience and confidence in their artistic journey.
</span>
</font>
