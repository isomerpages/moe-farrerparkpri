<br>
<span style="font-size:16px;">
Through the STELLAR (Strategies for English Language Learning and Reading) programme, students are immersed in language learning using a wide range of age-appropriate and engaging texts. 
With teaching processes such as the Modified Language Experience Approach (MLEA), students apply their learning in authentic contexts that make language learning fun.
</span>
<br>
<strong>Modified Language Experience Approach (MLEA)</strong><br>
<strong>Postcard Writing Activity</strong><br>
<span style="font-size:16px;">
As part of Primary 2 Unit 13 Postcards to David, students wrote postcards and shared their work with their parents.
</span>
<br>
<strong>Sea Aquarium Learning Journey</strong><br>
<span style="font-size:16px;">
Primary 2 students visited the S.E.A. Aquarium, using their experiences as inspiration for writing.
</span>

<strong><u>Programmes</u></strong><br>
<span style="font-size:16px;">
Our English programmes develop students’ listening, reading, speaking, and writing skills through meaningful learning experiences.
</span>
<br>
<strong>1. Supporting Lower Progress Learners</strong>
<div style="height:4px;"></div>
<span style="font-size:16px;">
Intervention programmes such as the Learning Support Programme (LSP), School-based Dyslexia Remediation (SDR), and Reading Remediation Programme (RRP) aim to support every learner. 
Students build strong literacy foundations through focused lessons in phonemic awareness and reading comprehension.
</span>
<div style="height:6px;"></div>
<strong>2. KidsREAD</strong>
<div style="height:4px;"></div>
<span style="font-size:16px;">
Our KidsREAD programme supports students with emerging literacy skills.
</span>
<br>

<span style="font-size:16px;">
Through weekly reading sessions led by parents, students develop a love of reading, cultivate positive reading habits, and gain confidence in language use.
</span>
<br>
<strong>3. Stretching High Progress Learners</strong><br>

<div align="center">
<img style="width:40%; max-width:420px;" src="/images/Year%202026/English/Picture8.png">
</div>
<br>
<span style="font-size:16px;">
We provide opportunities for students to challenge themselves and shine beyond the classroom through online storytelling competitions and debate, helping them grow into confident and articulate communicators.
</span>
<br>
<strong>4. Applied Learning Programme (English)</strong><br>
<span style="font-size:16px;">
Our Applied Learning Programme (ALP) provides authentic contexts for students to use English meaningfully.
Through real-world tasks, students develop presentation skills and learn to represent their ideas digitally.
</span>
