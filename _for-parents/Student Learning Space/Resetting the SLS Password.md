#### How to self reset SLS Password

<img src="/images/sls3.png" style="width:60%">
 
 Click on the Forget Password at the log in page of Student Learning Space.
 
 <img src="/images/sls4.png" style="width:60%">
 
 Enter your username and click submit
 
  <img src="/images/sls5.png" style="width:60%">
	
	Choose the option to received reset link through email.

If you did not setup your email for password reset or you have no access to your email, choose the next option to reset password by answering security questions.
