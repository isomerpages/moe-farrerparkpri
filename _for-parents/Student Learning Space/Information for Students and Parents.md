<h4 style="padding: 8px;">How the Singapore Student Learning Space Supports Learning</h4>

<ol style="padding: 15px 20px;">
  <li style="margin-bottom: 10px; font-size: 16px;">
    As part of MOEâ€™s commitment to nurturing future-ready learners, an online learning platform known as the Singapore Student Learning Space (SLS) was developed and launched in 2018.
  </li>
  <li style="margin-bottom: 10px; font-size: 16px;">
    The SLS provides curriculum-aligned resources and tools that support learning anytime, anywhere, enabling students to learn collaboratively and at their own pace. This encourages greater ownership of learning and builds lifelong learning habits.
  </li>
  <li style="margin-bottom: 10px; font-size: 16px;">
    Teachers use SLS to complement classroom teaching by designing meaningful lessons that promote exploration, collaboration, and self-directed learning.
  </li>
  <li style="font-size: 16px;">
    The SLS is shaped by feedback from both teachers and students, ensuring it remains dynamic and responsive to their evolving needs.
  </li>
</ol>
